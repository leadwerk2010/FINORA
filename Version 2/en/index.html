Questions about finances? There's plain text here.

Here you will find answers to the questions we are asked most frequently, clearly, honestly and without jargon.

How much does the consultation cost?

The costs depend on the scope, e.g. B. Depot check vs. complete strategy with implementation and support. You will receive one in the initial consultation transparent assessmentwhich steps make sense and what they cost.

How does Finora make money?

Transparent and understandable: You know what you are paying for and why a recommendation is made. We will discuss the details in the initial consultation, open and without small print.

Can the consultation take place in English?

Yes, advice is possible in German or English.

Do I have to make a decision immediately after the first conversation?

No. The initial consultation is no obligation. You gain clarity and decide calmly whether you want to take the next steps with us.
