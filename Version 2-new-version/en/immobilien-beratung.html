Case Study // Adrian

32 years · Software Engineer · first investment property

Adrian's Goals:

Adrian wanted three things: to set up his tax and wealth structure sensibly, to invest in real assets (as a supplement to the ETF portfolio) and to build long-term wealth for his family.

Adrian's Challenge:

Adrian knew: Real estate can be a strong building block. But between the job, platform research and property documents, it quickly became clear what was failing in practice: evaluation, time and security in the decision.

E

Lack of know-how

“How do I recognize a solid investment and how do I avoid expensive, bad purchases?”

E

High time expenditure

Checking objects, understanding documents, checking the plausibility of numbers - that eats up time if you do it on the side.

E

Fear of making wrong decisions

The more uncertainty, the greater the inhibition threshold until you end up not investing at all.

From analysis to Property

A good investment does not start with the advertisement, but with your situation: goals, liquidity, risk and tax framework. That's exactly how we proceeded with Adrian, first clarity, then object.

1

Analysis by Finora

Before we started looking for a suitable property together, we analyzed it Adrian's overall situation:

  • Tax situation, income structure, liquidity
  • His financial goals, time horizon and risk tolerance
  • Opportunities for tax optimization as part of the investment strategy
  • Sensible investment framework

Together we developed a tailor-made investment strategy that was both private and personal and financial situation.

2

The solution

Based on the strategy, we specifically identified properties, pre-screened them and only pursued those candidates who met Adrian's criteria. The tests were carried out according to clear standards:

  • Micro/macro location and rentability
  • Potential for long-term performance (conservatively viewed)
  • Building structure, condition and modernization history
  • Economic viability (rent, costs, buffers, scenarios)
3

Outcome

Adrian decided on his first investment property. In this specific case, among other things, the following effects:

  • Due to the tax structuring, the property was cash flow positive in the model calculation from day 1
  • We got started with €10,000 in equity (according to the planning at the time)
  • With conservative assumptions (e.g. 3% p. a. performance), the projection over 10 years showed a possible profit in the high five-digit range
  • Historical data on the situation has shown higher long-term performance, but this is not a guarantee for the future
4

Spoiler

The first property was just the start for Adrian. In the following two years, with our support, he expanded his portfolio to include two more properties.
