Future topics use in a structured manner

Thematic investing means not investing rigidly according to country/index weighting, but rather setting specific priorities where structural changes are taking place.

We classify these changes into four megatrend areas:

1. Technology & digitalization

AI, cyber security, automation, where productivity and infrastructure grow.

2. Urbanization & Infrastructure

Cities, networks, supply: investments that reflect real needs.

3. Resources & Supply

Efficiency, alternatives, circulation: when scarcity forces innovation.

4. Demographics & social change

Aging societies, healthcare, new consumption patterns and growing middle classes.

For us, professional portfolio management means: understanding trends, translating them into investable components and controlling the portfolio in a structured manner, with diversification, risk budget and rebalancing instead of “market timing”.

Real estate & tax structure

If real estate suits your situation, it can sensibly combine wealth creation and tax effects, provided the strategy, property and financing are carefully thought out.

Depending on your personal situation and property, investment properties can offer tax effects (e.g. via depreciation) and bring real material value into the portfolio. What is crucial is: conservative calculation instead of wishful thinking.

How this works:

Together with our partner Relago Project Development GmbH We develop a real estate strategy that fits into your overall portfolio, with a focus on stability, understandable assumptions and tax classification within the legal framework.
