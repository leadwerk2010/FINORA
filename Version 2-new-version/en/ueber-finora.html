Why advice at all?

Because the crucial questions are not “Which product?” but “Which plan?” are: goals, risk, structure, costs, implementation, and good decisions when markets fluctuate. This is exactly what Finora helps with.

How much does the consultation cost?

That depends on the scope. In the initial consultation you will receive a transparent assessment of which steps make sense and what they will cost.

How does Finora make money?

Transparent and comprehensible. You know what you are paying for and why a recommendation is being made.

Can the consultation take place in English?

Yes. You can receive advice in German or English.

Do I have to make a decision immediately after the first conversation?

No. The initial consultation is no obligation. You gain clarity and decide calmly.
